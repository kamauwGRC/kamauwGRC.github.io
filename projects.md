## Donation Portal Fraud Mitigation & Bot Defense

**Fraud Mitigation** | S.I.D.M | 11/2022 – 11/2022

Detected and mitigated a large-scale credit card testing attack on the donation portal, implementing robust rate-limiting and access controls to secure financial assets.

**Focus:** Securing public-facing web infrastructure against automated credit card testing and fraudulent payment activity

### Approach
Identified a surge in anomalous traffic on the organization’s donation portal indicative of "carding" attacks, where bad actors were utilizing the page to validate stolen credit card numbers. I executed an immediate incident response by documenting attack vectors, blacklisting malicious IP ranges, and collaborating with a third-party security consultant to implement rate-limiting and timed entry controls that neutralized automated abuse.

### Key Deliverables
- Documented the scope of unauthorized traffic and the specific patterns of attempted fraudulent transactions
- Implemented proactive IP blocking strategies to neutralize active malicious actor
- Established ongoing monitoring protocols to detect and flag high-frequency failure rates at the payment gateway level.

**Methods:** Incident Response | Threat Monitoring | Web Security | Access Control Engineering
